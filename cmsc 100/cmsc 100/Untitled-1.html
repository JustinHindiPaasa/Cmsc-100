Autobiography

My name is Justin Andrei R. Ibanez, and I was born on May 25, 2003, in Calamba, Laguna. From a young age, I had a natural curiosity and eagerness to learn, especially when it came to computers and mathematics.

During my childhood, I spent much of my time playing with toys and video games, a passion that remains with me to this day.

In my early school years, I attended Trace College, where I felt comfortable, partly because of the air conditioning, and also because I had the opportunity to learn languages like Chinese and Spanish. However, due to financial constraints, my parents could no longer afford the tuition, and I had to transfer to Los Baños Elementary School, a public school. The transition was difficult, and I found myself crying because I didn't know anyone, and the lack of air conditioning was uncomfortable to what I was used to.

The cultural shift was overwhelming, especially since I was placed in a pilot section, which presented its own challenges. My 3rd and 4th grades were tough, and I didn't make it to the honor roll. However, in 5th grade, I worked hard and managed to secure 10th place in my section, out of over 300 students. This achievement allowed me to return to Trace College, where I was able to prove my capabilities and earn a scholarship.

During my high school years at Trace College, I fully focused on academics and consistently made it to the honor roll, excelling particularly in math and computer science.

After graduating with a STEM track, I am eying on the University of the Philippines (UP), with applying the mindset "UP or nothing". To my great efforts, I was accepted into my dream course at my dream school.
